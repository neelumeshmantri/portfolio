Differential Heat Equation in n-Dimensions

IIT Madras Research Science Initiative (RSI)

Duration: Apr 2025 - Jun 2025

Description: Comprehensive research project examining the derivation, analytical solutions, and practical applications of the partial differential heat equation across multiple dimensions. The project bridges theoretical mathematics with computational implementations.

Key Components:

  • Rigorous mathematical derivation of heat equation in n-dimensional space
  • Analytical solution techniques including separation of variables and Fourier series
  • Numerical methods implementation using Python (NumPy, SciPy, Matplotlib)
  • Visualization and simulation using Wolfram Mathematica
  • Applications in thermodynamics, material science, and engineering

Outcome: Successfully completed as part of top 40 selection among 800+ applicants. Project received guidance from eminent IIT Madras faculty including Dr V Balakrishnan and Dr Mangala Sunder Krishnan.

Technologies: Python, Wolfram Mathematica, LaTeX, Partial Differential Equations, Numerical Analysis

Quantum Algorithm Research

Grover's Algorithm for Optimization Problems

Presented at AIMER Conference 2025

Research Focus: Investigation into the applicability of Grover's quantum search algorithm for solving various optimization problems, with particular emphasis on Oracle design and implementation.

Research Areas:

  • Unstructured search problems in large datasets
  • Boolean satisfiability (SAT) problems
  • Combinatorial optimization
  • Oracle construction for different problem domains
  • Quantum speedup analysis and benchmarking

Methodology: Theoretical analysis combined with practical implementations using Qiskit on IBM Quantum computers. Evaluated performance against classical algorithms across various problem sizes.

Findings: Demonstrated quadratic speedup for specific problem classes, identified optimal Oracle designs, and established limitations for real-world applications on current NISQ devices.

Technologies: Qiskit, IBM Quantum, Python, Quantum Computing, Algorithm Analysis
